Pasta with salmon

Salmon with Pasta is a very fast to make recipe, fat free, delicious and super healthy

  • 1. Cook the pasta according to the directions on the package, putting the salmon in the water six minutes before the cooking time is up.
  • 2. Preheat the oil in a pan and toast the pine nuts. Fry the pepper until it becomes soft. Add a ladleful of the pasta water and stir until the mushrooms begin to soften.
  • 3. After the pasta is cooked, drain the pasta and place the salmon on a plate. Using a fork, flake the salmon and combine it with the veggies, pasta, and basil leaves. Before serving, add a little pepper and toss everything together.

stir-fried Chicken

"Stir-fried Chicken, is a very nutrious meal that will only take you 10-15 minutes to make!"

  • 1. Prepare the rice as directed on the package, then drain. In a nonstick wok set over high heat, fry the red onions and ginger for two minutes. Cook for one minute after adding the carrots, broccoli stem, and chilli, if using.
  • 2. Add the broccoli florets and 3 tablespoons water after briefly stir-frying the chicken and cumin. Once the chicken is cooked through and the broccoli florets are just tender, cover and steam for 3/4 minutes.
  • 3. In the interim, combine the vinegar, tamari, and peanut butter. Serve the chicken and vegetables with the sauce mixed in, atop cooked rice.
