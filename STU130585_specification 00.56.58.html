Mini Site Specification

Objective/Goals

The purpose of the mini-site is to offer and encourage a wide range of people to an active and healthy lifestyle. Our objective is to provide a diverse range of captivating content that inspires users to take up new hobbies, participate in physical activities, and expand their intellectual perspectives.

Key Audience(s)

The key audience for this mini-site includes individuals of all ages who are interested in improving their physical fitness, exploring new activities, and challenging themselves intellectually. We aim to cater to a diverse audience, including fitness enthusiasts, hobbyists, and lifelong learners.

Provisional Site Structure

The mini-site will consist of the following content pages and a feedback form:

  • 1. Home Page: Welcoming visitors and providing an overview of the mini-site's offerings.
  • 2. Brain Teasers: Engaging users with brain-training games.
  • 3. Healthy Recipes: Providing a collection of nutritious recipes and cooking tips.
  • 4. Success Stories: Showcasing inspiring stories of individuals who have improved their health and intellect through the mini-site.
  • 5. Contact Us: A feedback form for users to provide comments, suggestions, and inquiries.

Functional Specification

The mini-site will offer the following key features:

  • 1. Navigation Menu: An intuitive menu for easy navigation between pages.
  • 2. Interactive Content: Engaging fitness challenges, brain teasers, and interactive quizzes with solutions and explanations.
  • 3. User Registration (Optional): Visitors can register to track their progress, save their favorite content, and receive personalized recommendations.
  • 4. Feedback Form: A user-friendly contact form for users to submit feedback, suggestions, and inquiries.
  • 5. Responsive Design: Ensuring the site is accessible and functional on various devices and screen sizes.

Competitor Analysis

We have conducted a competitive analysis of similar websites in the health, fitness, and education domains. Our mini-site aims to differentiate itself by:

  • 1. Combining Physical and Intellectual Content: Unlike competitors, we provide a unique blend of physical fitness challenges and intellectual activities, offering a holistic experience.
  • 2. Personalized User Experience: Through optional user registration, we aim to provide personalized content recommendations and progress tracking, enhancing user engagement.
  • 3. Real-Life Success Stories: We highlight real success stories of individuals who have benefited from our mini-site, inspiring and motivating our users.
  • 4. Cross-Browser Compatibility: Our site is designed to work seamlessly across various browsers, ensuring accessibility for a wider audience.
